The following types of organizations are **PROHIBITED** from using the Software:

- **Weapon Manufacturers**: Organizations that design, produce, or distribute weapons, munitions, or military hardware

- **Military Intelligence**: Organizations engaged in military intelligence, surveillance, or reconnaissance operations
- **Private Military Companies**: Private military contractors, mercenary organizations, or security contractors engaged in combat operations

### 1.3 Indirect Military Use

Organizations whose **primary business** involves any of the following are prohibited from using the Software:

- Supplying components, materials, or services to organizations engaged in prohibited activities listed in sections 1.1 or 1.2
- Providing research and development services that directly support military hardware or weapons systems

- Providing consulting, engineering, or technical services for military applications

### 1.4 Permitted Civilian Use

The following remain **PERMITTED** uses:

- **Civilian Research**: Academic research, scientific studies, and educational purposes with no military application
- **Public Safety**: Civilian emergency services, disaster response, medical services, and public health applications
- **Commercial Applications**: Procedural content generation for entertainment, gaming, simulations without military context
